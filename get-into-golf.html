Insights

Key recommendations

Expand GiG provision

  • 606 venues (33.5%) have zero GiG opportunities. Primary targets for outreach.
  • Holiday camps at just 135 venues. School holidays are a prime window for junior engagement.
  • Family golf is almost invisible (44 opportunities). Family-friendly formats could be a significant growth lever.

Regional priorities

  • Junior gap regions: Cornwall (40.6%), Merseyside (41.7%), Nottinghamshire (43.8%), Bedfordshire (50.0%)
  • Women's programme gaps: Cornwall (9.4%), Bedfordshire (11.1%), Cumbria (11.1%), Shropshire (13.6%)
  • 448 venues already offer dedicated women's programmes. Strong opportunity to expand further.

Improve digital presence

  • 63.9% of GiG opportunities lack published pricing. Price transparency is critical for new golfers.
  • 50.4% have no specific schedule. "Contact venue" is a significant barrier for someone considering trying golf.
  • Better online information directly reduces friction for first-time participants.
